The Good Old Days

DIRECTOR : Bat-Amgalan, Ian Allardyce

Mongolia / 14'30''

SYNOPSIS

A lone horseman travels a harsh and ruinous future landscape. The precious fuel he carries attracts the attention of an unwanted traveling companion. In this world of depleted resources we consider whether friendship and morality still outmatch fear, greed and the force of violence.

YEAR 2013
