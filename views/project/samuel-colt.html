SAMUEL COLT

SYNOPSIS

Samuel Colt is a supernatural thriller with similarities to films like The Devil's Advocate or Limitless. The main character undergoes an extreme carpe diem journey after realizing that selling your soul to the devil in order to accomplish your dreams is the fastest way to turn your life into a living nightmare. "God created man, Samuel Colt made them equal.”

>

Director: Oleguer Homs

Oleguer Homs has published a scifi Catalan novel called “The Promised Land” in 2012. He has studied Audivisual Communications at UVIC, a master in Film Directing at ESCAC, and Film and TV Writing at UCLA Extension.
