SYNOPSIS

Julien (19) arrives unannounced at his brother Max’s place. Max (28) lives in a travellers’ camp in the middle of the Forêt Cévenole. The brothers have not seen each other for several years. Julien falls in love with Lise, a casual lover of Max’s. Lise, who also lives in the community, makes music with Max. This messy love upsets Julien and causes between the brothers unresolved family conflicts to resurface.

Director: Fred Gélard

Fred Gélard is an actor, director and screenwriter. Formed in Beaux-arts school and National Conservatory of Dramatique Art in Rennes, he begins in movies in 1991, plays the main character of "Beau fixe" by Christian Vincent and "Fast" by Dante Desarthe in 1993 and 1994. He continues to play in Cinéma and begins to work for télévision from 1999. He begins as director in 1996 with a first short movie "Où tu vas" in which he plays and who will be programme on Arte the following year. Then he made two others shorts movies "Ma vie active" and "Dans mon île" both programmed on TPS and France 3 and primed in severals festivals. "Free party" is his fourth film. He works on two feature films at the moment.
