SYNOPSIS

Anna has a man, who she loves, on the one hand and a beloved mother on the other. An insane connection with her mother, which does not let Anna live on her own, ruins the relationship with her boyfriend, because she got stuck in the past.

Anya a sa mère bien-aimée et son homme bien-aimé. L`affection folie a sa mère laquelle ne veut pas laisser sa fille dérange des relations avec cet homme et ne laisse aucune chance à l'héroïne sur la vie indépendante.

About the filmmaker

Director: Daria Razumnikova

Born in Moscow. Initially she studied as a marketing specialist. She worked as an art director assistant at the theatre «Praktika», and also at «Textura» festival, press relations service. In 2012 she graduated from the State University – Higher School of Economics, fiction film directing workshop of Anna Fenchenko, Alexander Kott and Vladimir Kott.
