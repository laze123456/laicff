WHO CAN SUBMIT

All filmmakers, whether student or professional, independent or corporate, are equally welcomed

SUBMISSION PERIOD

AUGUST 22 - JANUARY 30

CATEGORIES

The 2017 LAICFF only accepts SHORTS under 40 minutes

All genres are welcomed

QUALIFICATION

Entries must meet at least ONE criterium from below:

  • Imbed certain local culture
  • Reflect certain cultural/social issue
  • Demonstrate local filmmaking style

A description specifying entry's connection to cultural or filmmaking diversity must be submitted with entry for the entry to be considered valid
